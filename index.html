  • Personal Lists Website - Flask, Python
    • 

    
    
    
        
        
    
    
    
    This website helps you keep personal
lists of music, movies, books, and TV shows.
I designed it using Figma/canva and developed
it with Flask/SQLAlchemy for the backend and
simple HTML/CSS for the frontend. Users can
rate and review books, movies, and TV shows,
and add Spotify links for songs. Data for
each element including its name, rating, and
review or the name, artist and link to
spotify incase of song is stored in separate
databases. User can also update and delete data
at any point after it has become part of the 
database. The site analyzes the data based
on ratings and the frequency of songs by
specific artists to provide top picks in all
categories. It offers a compact and
well-organized way to curate your lists.
As a lists enthusiast, I wanted to have all
my lists in one place, and this website was
created to fulfill that need. I hope you
enjoy it as well! I'm also planning to
evolve it further to include more data
analysis, maybe an option for genre etc.
